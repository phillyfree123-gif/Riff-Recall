Riff & Recall

🎮 How To Play

Riff and Recall is a 3-round music trivia game. One player hosts on a TV — everyone else joins on their phones. The host controls the game; players buzz in their answers.

Round 1 — Audio Clips: A song plays. Listen carefully and pick the correct artist from 4 options. Be fast — speed matters!

Round 2 — Who Is This?: A photo of a musician appears on the TV. Identify them from 4 choices. Watch the TV screen — not your phone!

Round 3 — Lyric Clues: A description of an iconic song appears. Guess the song and artist. The clue is on your phone too!

🏆 Scoring

In every round, speed and accuracy are rewarded. The first correct answer scores the most points. Everyone who gets it right earns points — but first place gets the glory!
